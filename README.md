# Daybreak — your daily operating system

A single-file dashboard to keep your days on rails: wake up, move, eat right,
knock out what matters, and watch the streak build. No accounts, no installs,
no internet required. Your data lives in your browser on this machine.

## How to use it

1. Open **`index.html`** by double-clicking it (or drag it into Chrome/Edge).
2. Bookmark it / pin the tab so it's one click every morning.
3. Each day:
   - **Start the day** — tap the big morning button when you're up and moving.
     It quietly logs the time (no targets, no bedtimes — that was the deal).
   - **Morning Engine** — check off your **run** (log miles/min if you want)
     and your **lift** (jot the split, e.g. "push day").
   - **Fuel** — four fast taps: protein, ate clean, hydrated, no junk.
   - **The Big 3** — type today's three non-negotiables and check them off.
     Dump everything smaller into **Also today** and tag it Work / Startup /
     Social / Personal so the summer-vs-startup-vs-job balance stays visible.
   - **Momentum** — your workout streak, diet streak, and a 5-week heatmap.
     Click any past square to view or backfill that day.

The ring up top fills as you complete the day. Consistency beats intensity —
a forgiving "either cardio or lift" keeps the workout streak alive on busy days.

## Your data

- Everything saves automatically to this browser (localStorage).
- **Backup data** downloads a `.json` file — do this once a week or before
  clearing your browser. **Restore** loads it back (also moves your history to a
  new computer or phone).
- **Clear this day** wipes only the day you're viewing.

## Heads up

- Data is per-browser/per-device. Use Backup/Restore to move between devices.
- Clearing browser site data will erase it — keep a backup.

Built to be lived in, not fussed over. Show up daily.
